Cannabis (Marijuana)

Status: Schedule I Controlled Substance

Federally illegal under the Controlled Substances Act. However, the 2018 Farm Bill legalized hemp (cannabis with less than 0.3% THC). Federal enforcement has generally deferred to state laws in states with legalization.

Psilocybin (Magic Mushrooms)

Status: Schedule I Controlled Substance

Federally illegal with no accepted medical use. Recent FDA breakthrough therapy designations for depression treatment have sparked renewed research interest.

DMT & Ayahuasca

Status: Schedule I Controlled Substance

Federally illegal. However, ayahuasca used in certain religious ceremonies by recognized churches may be exempt under the Religious Freedom Restoration Act.

Peyote (Mescaline)

Status: Schedule I Controlled Substance

Federally illegal, but the American Indian Religious Freedom Act allows use by Native American Church members for religious purposes.
